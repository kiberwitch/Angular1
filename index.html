New Environmental Regulations Impacting Freight Industry
Tomas Ericson
February 27, 2025
Governments worldwide are tightening emissions regulations for the shipping and trucking sectors, aiming to reduce carbon footprints and promote sustainability.
The International Maritime Organization (IMO) has introduced stricter fuel efficiency standards, requiring vessels to cut emissions significantly by 2030. Similarly, several countries, including the US and EU nations, are enforcing new carbon taxes and emissions reporting requirements for freight companies.
To stay compliant, logistics providers are investing in alternative fuels, electric trucks, and energy-efficient shipping routes.
